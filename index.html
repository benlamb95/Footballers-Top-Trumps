How do I play?

Both you and the computer start with an 8 card deck with random football players within them.


You must then select a statistic from either; Attacking, Midfield, Defending and Goalkeeping which you think will have the higher amount than the computers card.


If your chosen stat is higher you will win the computers card, however if you dont the computer will take yours.


The winner will be the player with all 16 cards!


Good Luck!
